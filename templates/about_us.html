About Tempest Wash Co

Santa Monica-Founded, LA-Driven, Eco-Focused Pressure Washing

Our Story

Tempest Wash Co was born in Santa Monica with one goal: to elevate the standard of pressure washing across Southern California. What started as a two-person operation has grown into a trusted, eco-conscious company serving dozens of communities in Los Angeles and Orange County. We built our reputation on transparent pricing, premium service, and a deep respect for the homes and businesses we serve.

What We Clean

From modern homes in West LA to historic storefronts in Pasadena, we specialize in:

  • Driveways, sidewalks & walkways
  • Patios, decks & pool areas
  • Home exteriors & stucco walls
  • Roof soft washing & gutter clearing
  • Commercial storefronts & signage
  • Graffiti removal & stain lifting

Core Values We Live By

  • Sustainability: All products are biodegradable & eco-friendly
  • Community: Locally owned & invested in SoCal neighborhoods
  • Quality: Trained, background-checked technicians
  • Trust: Fully insured with a 100% satisfaction guarantee
