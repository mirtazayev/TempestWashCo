About Tempest Wash Co

Los Angeles' Trusted Name in Eco-Friendly Pressure Washing

Our Story

Tempest Wash Co was founded by two passionate professionals on a mission to bring clean, safe, and sustainable pressure washing to homes and businesses across all of Los Angeles County and Orange County. From Santa Monica to Sherman Oaks, we’ve built a reputation for quality, care, and green practices.

Why Choose Tempest Wash Co?

  • Fully insured & locally owned in Santa Monica
  • Friendly, trained, background-checked technicians
  • Only biodegradable & non-toxic products
  • Fast, flexible scheduling 7 days a week
  • 100% satisfaction guarantee on every job

Areas We Serve

We proudly serve all of Los Angeles County and parts of Orange County including: Santa Monica, Los Angeles, Inglewood, Sherman Oaks, Culver City, Beverly Hills, Pasadena, Long Beach, Torrance, West LA, Marina del Rey, Orange County, Irvine, Anaheim, Anaheim and more.

What We Clean

Whether it’s a driveway in Inglewood, a patio in West Hollywood, or roof soft washing in Irvine, we’ve got it covered. We specialize in residential & commercial pressure washing, including storefronts, fences, stucco walls, and graffiti removal.
