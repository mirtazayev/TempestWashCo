How to Estimate Your Property Size

Use these simple tips to measure or estimate the sizes of the areas you want cleaned. Don’t worry about being exact — just your best guess helps us provide a better quote!

1. House Exterior

  • Estimate the total square footage of your home’s exterior walls for pressure washing.
  • For a 1-story house, use the footprint size or the area of the walls.
  • For a 2-story house, add the first and second floors together.
  • If you don’t know, use your home’s total square footage from property records or real estate listings as a rough guide.

2. Driveways & Patios

  • Measure the length and width in feet, then multiply to get square footage (length × width).
  • If measuring is tricky, pace out the area: one adult step ≈ 2.5 feet.
  • For irregular shapes, break the area into smaller rectangles or circles and sum their areas.
  • If your driveway is less than 400 sq.ft., it qualifies for our flat rate pricing.

3. Sidewalks & Walkways

  • Estimate either the length in linear feet or the total square footage if the sidewalk is wide.
  • If you only have length, that's fine—we can calculate approximate area based on standard widths.

4. Fence & Gutters

  • Measure or estimate the total linear feet of fence or gutters you want cleaned.
  • Include all sides and sections you want done.

5. Roof

  • Estimate the total roof area in square feet.
  • If you don’t know, use your home’s square footage as a rough guide (roof area is usually about 1.5 × home footprint).
  • We use a soft wash to safely remove algae and mold.
